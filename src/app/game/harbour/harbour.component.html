- MOVES -
  • You can click on your own quarry to retrieve up to 3 stones (bottom left).
  • If you have enough stones on your supply sled, you can click on the "+" button on a ship to place there one of your own stones.
  • When a ship becomes selectable, to move it towards a dock you have to first select it and then click on the dock (red dot) of your choosing
  • To play a card you have to double click it.
  • When a stone is placed, you can see it highlighted in yellow directly on the ship.
