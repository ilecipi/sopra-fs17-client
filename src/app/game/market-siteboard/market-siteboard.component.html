MARKET
  • For each stone delivered, the owner of the stone can immediately take any 1 card.
  • Pick order is decided by the order of the stones on the ship.
  • All other players can see which card you have.
  • Red cards are played immediately.
  • Blue cards can be played in a later turn of the player who draws it.
  • Green and purple cards will earn you points at the end of the game
